Maidenhead Judo Club

A local, friendly, judo club

For those who want to take part in competitive judo or just want to have fun learning an Olympic sport in a safe environment.

  • Improve balance and coordination
  • Increase strength and fitness
  • Gain confidence
  • Learn a skill for life
  • Compete in competition

  • Youth (14+) & Adults: 7.30-9pm
